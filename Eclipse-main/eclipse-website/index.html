The Journey

Roadmap to
Totality

A 5-year vision. Six release series. Each one a calculated step toward building something that's never existed before.

DAWN 0.1 – 0.5 · Completed
Polish & Stabilize
Fix, refine, and perfect the foundation. Splash screen, greeting system, menu expansion, logo integration, and removal of dead code.
UMBRA 1.0 – 1.5 · Now
Core Features
SearXNG search integration, AI Answer Mode via Groq, custom search card UI, and first public launch on the Play Store.
PENUMBRA 2.0 – 2.4 · 6–12 months
Full Customization
Drag-and-drop home screen layout, widget system, community themes, glassmorphism mode, and deep personalization engine.
CORONA 3.0 – 3.4 · 1–1.5 years
Community Layer
Theme marketplace, shared layouts, user profiles, community-created widgets, and social browsing features.
TOTALITY 4.0 – 4.3 · 1.5–3 years
Independent Search
Eclipse's own search engine. Custom crawling, ranking, and result display — not dependent on any third-party engine.
SIRIUS 5.0 – 5.2 · 3–5 years
Multi-Platform & Beyond
Desktop, iOS, voice control, launcher mode, and advanced AI integration. The full Eclipse ecosystem.
